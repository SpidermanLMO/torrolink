Don't write thin keyword-stuffed articles. Write genuinely useful guides like:

- "The Complete Guide to QR Codes for Local Businesses" (pillar page)
- "How [Restaurant Type] Use QR Codes to Get More Repeat Customers"

One great article ranks better than 20 mediocre ones.
