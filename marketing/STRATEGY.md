# Torrolink — Marketing Strategy
## PTorro Holdings LLC | 2026

---

## The Big Picture

Torrolink sells QR codes to local small businesses. But the real product is **trust and discoverability** — a scannable bridge between a physical business and a digital presence, at a price any small business can afford.

**Official slogan (use this everywhere — social bios, ads, pitches, print):**
> "One QR code. Your whole business in a scan."

We're not competing with Linktree (which targets influencers). We're targeting the restaurant owner who hands out menus, the plumber who leaves business cards, the food truck that wants foot traffic to follow them on Instagram.

---

## Target Customers

### Primary (highest conversion)
- Restaurants and food trucks
- Trades: plumbers, electricians, HVAC, contractors
- Hair salons, barbershops, nail salons
- Auto repair shops
- Fitness studios and personal trainers
- Retail boutiques

### Secondary (longer sales cycle but higher LTV)
- Real estate agents
- Wedding/event vendors (photographers, DJs, caterers)
- Healthcare: chiropractors, dentists, massage therapists
- Cleaning and lawn care services

### Why these? They all share:
- Physical customer touchpoints (they hand things to people)
- No in-house web team
- Value reviews, repeat customers, and referrals
- $28–$47 is trivially affordable vs. the leads it generates

## Pricing Psychology

Our prices ($28.33, $37.61, $46.61) are intentionally specific — not rounded. Specific numbers signal precision and credibility, not a generic product. Use this in copy: *"Starting at $28.33 — not $30, not $29.99. We priced it to the penny so you know exactly where every dollar goes."*

## Channel Strategy

### 1. Organic Social (Start here — zero cost)

**Instagram/Facebook**: Document the journey. Post real businesses using Torrolink. Before/after: "Mike's Plumbing had a plain business card. Now every card has a QR that shows his reviews, booking link, and intro video."

**TikTok/Reels**: Short demos. "Scan this QR code. Here's what a customer sees." Film on your phone. Raw and real beats polished.

**Target hashtags**: #smallbusiness #localbusiness #qrcode #businesstips #restaurantowner #shoplocal #tradesman

Post cadence: 5x/week minimum for 60 days to build momentum.

---

### 2. Local Business Outreach (Highest ROI)

**In-person cold walk-ins**: The most underrated channel for this product. Walk into a restaurant, hair salon, or auto shop and say: *"I run a service that puts your whole business on a QR code for less than $30. Scan this."* Show them a live demo profile. Close on the spot or leave a card.

Target 10 businesses per day in a 5-mile radius. At a 10% close rate, that's 1 sale/day = $30+/day from walking.

**Local Facebook Groups**: Every city has "Support Local [City]" groups with thousands of members. Post genuinely helpful content about QR codes for small business, mention Torrolink naturally. Do not spam — add value first.

**Chambers of Commerce**: Join your local chamber. Sponsor one meeting or event. One intro at a chamber of commerce can lead to 10 referrals.

---

### 3. Cold Email (Scalable)

Target: business owners with Google My Business listings but no website (easy to find, high need).

Subject lines that work:
- "Quick question about [Business Name]'s online presence"
- "Saw your shop on Google — had an idea"
- "This took 2 minutes and made [Local Business] 3x more scannable"

See `outreach/cold-email-template.md` for full scripts.

---

### 4. Content / SEO (6-12 month play)

Target keywords with real buying intent:
- "qr code for small business"
- "qr code menu restaurant"
- "qr code business card"
- "how to make a qr code for my business"

Write genuinely useful articles. Don't write for Google — write for the plumber who googles "how do I get more calls from my business card." Rank for that. Convert at the bottom.

See `content/seo-keywords.md` and `content/blog-post-ideas.md`.

---

### 5. Affiliate / Referral Program (Medium term)

Structure:
- Refer a business that buys → $10 cash or $10 credit
- Agencies/consultants who refer 5+ clients/month → 20% recurring on metrics plans

Target affiliates:
- Website designers and developers (they work with exactly our customers)
- Marketing consultants who serve small businesses
- Business coaches
- Accountants and bookkeepers (they talk to every small biz owner)

Start with a simple tracking link and Venmo/PayPal payout. Formalize later.

---

### 6. Paid Ads (When cash flow allows)

**Facebook/Instagram Ads**: Best for this product. Target by job title (business owner, restaurant owner, self-employed), location (your city or nationwide), interests (small business).

Starting budget: $20/day. Test 3 creatives. Kill losers fast, scale winners.

Best performing creative type for this product: **video demo** — show someone scanning a QR code and the profile page loading. No words needed.

**Google Ads**: Target "qr code for business" keywords. Higher CPCs but higher intent.

Do NOT run paid ads until you have 5+ organic sales. Proof of concept first.
