# TorroLink — Marketing Strategy
**BRAND: TorroLink (PTorro Holdings)**
Last updated: 2026-06-20

---

## Who We're Selling To

**Primary:** Local small business owners who have physical customer traffic.

Best-fit businesses:
- Restaurants, cafés, food trucks
- Salons, barbershops, nail salons, spas
- Contractors (roofing, plumbing, HVAC, electrical, landscaping)
- Retail shops, boutiques, gift shops
- Auto repair, detailing
- Gyms, yoga studios, martial arts

**What they have in common:**
- Customers physically visit their location
- They lose customers who want to follow them or leave a review but can't do it easily
- They're not tech-heavy — simple is everything
- They're price-sensitive (our pricing needs to feel like a no-brainer)

**Not a good fit:**
- Fully online businesses (no physical touchpoint)
- Enterprise or franchise locations (too complex for our current product)

---

## The Core Message

**Headline:** One QR code. Everything your customers need.

**What we're selling:** Not a QR code. We're selling the moment after a customer visits — making it effortless for them to follow, review, save, and come back.

**Why now:** Every business has a Google profile and social media. None of them make it easy for walk-in customers to connect with all of it in one place. TorroLink is that one place.

## Channels (Priority Order)

1. **TikTok** — @lmorros — product demos, founder story, SMB use cases. Low follower count now but highest organic reach potential. Post 3x/week minimum.
2. **Facebook** — laign.orros.7 — local business groups, direct outreach, community presence. Best for reaching SMB owners in [CITY].
3. **Instagram** — @laign_o — visual demos, carousels, reels. Secondary to TikTok.
4. **Cold email** — hello@torrolink.com — targeted SMB outreach. See cold-smb-sequence.md.
5. **In-person** — walk-ins to local businesses. Highest conversion rate. Laign does this directly.
6. **LinkedIn** — laign-orros — founder credibility, B2B angles.

---

## Launch Campaign (Activate When Site Goes Live)

**Week 1:** 5 social posts across channels. Walk-in cold calls to 10 local businesses/day. Post the launch announcement email to early list.
**Week 2:** Join 3 local Facebook groups. Cold email 20 businesses. Start the cold-smb-sequence.
**Week 3:** Document first customer's before/after story. Post it everywhere.
**Week 4:** Case study live across all channels. Launch a referral offer: "Get 1 month free for every business you refer."

## Pricing (Current)

- Free tier: basic profile + QR code
- Paid tier: Metrics + Leads add-on (~$10.28/month)
- Marketing angle: "Free to start. Upgrade when you're ready to see who's scanning."

---

## Key Metrics to Watch (Oracle tracks these)

- QR scans per profile (daily, WoW, MoM)
- Free → paid conversion rate
- Churn rate (subscribers cancelling)
- Email open rates and reply rates from cold outreach
- Social reach and follower growth

---

## What's NOT in This Strategy

- Paid ads — not yet. Organic first. Ads come after we have case studies and proof.
- Influencer marketing — not the right channel for SMB owners.
- PR — too early. Focus on direct sales first.
