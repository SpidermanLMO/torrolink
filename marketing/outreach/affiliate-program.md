# Torrolink — Affiliate & Referral Program

## Overview

Turn happy customers and industry partners into a sales force.
Every referral they make costs you only on success — no upfront marketing spend.

---

## Tier 1: Customer Referral Program

**Who:** Anyone who has purchased a Torrolink QR code

**How it works:**
- Each customer gets a unique referral link (e.g. torrolink.com?ref=mikesplumbing)
- When someone purchases through their link, they earn $10
- Payout via PayPal, Venmo, or credit toward a Metrics upgrade

**Why it works:**
Customers who are happy with their QR code naturally show it off to other business owners. Make that referral have a dollar attached to it.

**Messaging to customers:**
> "Know another business owner who'd love this? Send them your link. You'll get $10 when they buy — no limit on how many people you refer."

---

## Tier 2: Partner Program

**Who:** Web designers, marketing consultants, bookkeepers, business coaches, virtual assistants

**How it works:**
- Partner gets a unique tracking link and 20% of every sale they refer
- On QR code sales: ~$5-9 per referral
- On Metrics plan: 20% recurring = $2.06/month per subscriber they referred
- Payout monthly via PayPal

**Why they'll do it:**
These people already work with small business owners. A $28 product recommendation with zero friction and a 20% cut is an easy upsell in any client conversation.

**Target partners:**
- Freelance web designers (they already build for small biz)
- Social media managers
- Marketing agencies serving local businesses
- SCORE mentors and small business coaches
- Accountants/bookkeepers (every small biz has one)

**Outreach to partners:**

Subject: "Want to add $200+/mo recommending something your clients actually need?"

"Hey [Name] — I run Torrolink, a QR code platform for local small businesses. It's $28 one-time, and I'm building a partner program where you earn 20% on every business you refer.

Given that you already work with [restaurants/contractors/salons], this is an easy recommendation. No selling required — just show them a demo link.

Want me to set you up with a partner link and a one-page overview you can share with clients?"

## Tier 3: Agency White Label (Future)

**Who:** Marketing agencies and digital consultants with 10+ local business clients

**How it works:**
- Agency resells Torrolink under their own brand at their own markup
- They pay wholesale, charge retail, keep the margin
- Torrolink handles all the tech, they handle the client relationship

**Pricing (when ready):**
- $20/code at 10+ per month
- $15/code at 50+ per month
- Custom pricing at 100+

This is a future revenue stream. Build the core product first, then approach agencies.

---

## Implementation (Phase 1 — Simple)

Before building a full affiliate system:

1. Create a Google Form for partners to sign up with their name, email, PayPal
2. Generate unique UTM links manually (e.g. torrolink.com?ref=partner-name)
3. Track in a spreadsheet: who referred who, when, how much owed
4. Pay out manually at end of each month

This is enough to validate the program before investing in automation.

Phase 2: Use a tool like Rewardful, Tapfiliate, or FirstPromoter to automate tracking and payouts.
