## Recommendation: Pflugerville, TX — FM 1825 / Pecan Street Corridor

**Why Pflugerville:**
- 14 miles NE of downtown Austin — Laign already operates in this zone (Waypoint Roofing referral network)
- One of the fastest-growing suburbs in Texas — population doubled in the last decade
- Dense concentration of trades and service businesses: HVAC, plumbing, cleaning, lawn care, auto repair
- Lower digital sophistication than central Austin — many businesses still running on Facebook-only or no web presence
- No need to travel far — already Laign's operating territory

**Why this beats central Austin:**
- Central Austin has more competition and higher web savviness
- Pflugerville / Manor business owners are more approachable, less bombarded by agencies
- Newer businesses (5–10 yrs old) that launched before web presence became table stakes

## How to Find the Targets (Beachhead's Approach)

1. **Google Maps scan:** Search "HVAC Pflugerville TX" → filter by no/bad website
2. **Street drive:** FM 1825 corridor — note shops without signs of digital presence
3. **Yelp check:** Businesses with 0–5 reviews and no website listed = prime targets
4. **Facebook check:** Business with Facebook-only presence = easy pitch ("You don't own that page")

**Signs of a target:**
- No website listed on Google Maps
- Website says "under construction" or links to a Wix site from 2015
- Only social media listed (Facebook/Instagram)
- Google Business Profile not claimed or half-filled
