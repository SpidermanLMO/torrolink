## Business Model

### The Sales Play
1. Identify 5 target businesses (no website or a bad one) in a specific area
2. **Mock-build their actual site** — real business name, real content, real photos
3. Walk in the door → show the owner their site on a laptop/tablet
4. Close the sale on the spot
5. Collect payment (Stripe link, same day)
6. Finalize and deploy that night
7. Repeat in the next block

**The pitch:** *"Get found on Google, get more calls — done in a week, for less than one job."*
For a plumber where one job = $800, a $529.28 website pays for itself in a single call.

## What Makes a Winning Site (Non-Negotiables)

1. **Loads under 2.5 seconds on mobile** (Core Web Vitals — affects Google ranking)
2. **Click-to-call above the fold** — most conversions for trades are phone calls
3. **3-field lead form max** — each extra field cuts submissions by ~11%
4. **Real photos** — stock photos kill trust; business owner's real work is the wow factor
5. **Trust signals** — reviews embedded, certifications, before/after
6. **Mobile-first layout** — 65% of visits are mobile
7. **Clear headline in first 5 seconds** — what you do, who you do it for, how to reach you
