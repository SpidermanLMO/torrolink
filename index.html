Questions answered

Frequently asked questions

Does my QR code ever expire?
No. Your Torrolink QR code is permanent. It never expires and never gets shut off — even if you never pay another cent after your one-time purchase. You own it, period.
Can I edit my profile page after I buy?
Yes, anytime. Log into your portal and update your logo, bio, links, phone number, or anything else. Changes go live immediately — no waiting, no approval process.
What happens if I cancel the Metrics plan?
Your QR code and profile page stay active — always. Canceling the $10.28/mo Metrics plan just removes scan analytics and the lead capture form. Your one-time purchase is permanent and nothing breaks.
Can I download my QR code for printing?
Yes. Download it as a PDF, JPEG, or SVG from your portal. All formats are print-ready and sized for truck wraps, yard signs, business cards, and flyers. Hand the SVG to any sign shop or wrap company and you are done.
Do I need technical skills to set this up?
None at all. After purchase you receive a login link by email. Setting up your profile — uploading a logo, adding your phone number and links — takes about 10 minutes. If you get stuck, email us at orders@torrolink.com and we respond same day.
Can I put the QR on my truck, yard signs, and business cards?
That is exactly what it is built for. Download the print-ready SVG and hand it to any sign shop or print house. The code scans perfectly whether it is 1 inch on a business card or 3 feet on a truck door.
What do I get right after I buy? How do I access my QR code?
Within minutes of purchase you will receive two emails: one with your login link to the portal, and one with your QR code image attached. Log into the portal to download additional formats (PDF, SVG, JPEG), set up your profile page, and manage everything from there. If you do not see the emails within a few minutes, check your spam folder or email orders@torrolink.com.
How does the lead capture form work?
When someone scans your QR and lands on your profile, they see a short contact form you customize — name, phone, email, and service checkboxes you set up (e.g. “Roof repair • Replacement • Storm damage”). When they hit Send, you get an instant email with their contact info. Lead capture requires the $10.28/mo Metrics & Leads plan, which you can add at checkout or activate anytime from your portal.
