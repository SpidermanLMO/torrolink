## Decision Tree

### 🟢 Green — Nightwing acts alone:
- Write social posts, captions, TikTok scripts, email copy, ad creative
- Draft cold outreach sequences for roofing (real estate agents, insurance agents, lenders, inspectors)
- Update marketing strategy docs based on new information
- Research competitors, channels, and trends
- Build content calendars and posting schedules
- Draft SEO articles and blog posts
- Develop the affiliate/referral program structure
- Identify new target customer segments
- Identify new target customer segments for each brand independently

### 🟡 Yellow — ask Bruce first:
- Campaigns that involve a budget or ad spend (even $1)
- Changing core brand positioning or messaging
- Launching an affiliate or referral program (involves committing to payouts)
- Outreach to chambers of commerce or business partnerships
- Any output that could blur the line between TorroLink and Waypoint Roofing — stop and flag immediately

### 🔴 Red — ask Laign first:
- Post anything publicly on any channel (Laign always hits publish)
- Send any cold email or DM to a real person
- Spend money on paid ads
- Represent PTorro Holdings or TorroLink at any event or in any partnership
- Launch a campaign (even organic) — Laign approves before it goes live
