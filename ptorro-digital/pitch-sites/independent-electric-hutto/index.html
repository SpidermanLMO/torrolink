Our Story

Independent Means You Pay for the Work, Not the Brand

Independent Electric is exactly what the name says — independent. No franchise fees, no corporate overhead, no inflated pricing to cover a call center. We're a locally licensed electrical contractor based in Hutto who treats every home like it's our own. When you call, you talk to the owner. When we show up, it's the owner. And when we quote a price, that's the price you pay.

Why choose Independent Electric over the competition?
Owner-direct pricing with no franchise overhead vs. chain electricians who add 30–40% for branding.
