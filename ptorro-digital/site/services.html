What it all means

Plain English. No jargon.

🔍

Basic SEO

When someone types "plumber Austin" or "HVAC near me" into Google, do they find you — or your competitor? SEO is what puts you on that list. We write your page titles, descriptions, and content the way Google reads them. Without it, your site is invisible.

📍

Google Business Profile

The box that pops up on the right side of Google with your hours, phone, reviews, and a "Get Directions" button. Shows on Google Maps. 84% of consumers find local businesses through Google. Without it, you don't show up on Maps at all.

📅

Booking Widget

A button on your site where customers pick a day and time and book directly — no phone tag, no "I'll call back later." They pick a slot, it goes to your calendar, you show up. For salons, trainers, cleaners — this alone pays for the site.

⭐

Reviews Integration

Pulls your Google reviews directly onto your website. 93% of consumers read reviews before hiring a local business. Showing them on your site means trust is built before they even hit "call." No going to Google — it's right there.

📞

Instant Lead Alert

When someone fills out your contact form, you get an email immediately — their name, what they need, their phone number. No logging in, no app. Just a notification so you can call back while they're still looking.

📊

Monthly Check-In

A quick report every month: how many people visited, where they came from, what they clicked. Did anyone fill out the form? Is your ranking moving? We tell you what's working and what to do next. Most businesses never know if their site is working — now you will.
