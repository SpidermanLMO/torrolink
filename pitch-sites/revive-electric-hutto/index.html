Our Story

Built for the Homes You Actually Live In

Derek Ruiz noticed something in 2019: the electricians in Hutto knew how to wire a house from 1985 but were scratching their heads at Tesla chargers and smart panels. He'd been in the trade since 18, kept up with every code update, and knew there was a market for an electrician who could handle what modern homes actually needed.

Revive Electric opened with zero marketing and grew entirely on referrals. Derek holds a Journeyman Electrician license, pulls every permit, and passes every inspection.
