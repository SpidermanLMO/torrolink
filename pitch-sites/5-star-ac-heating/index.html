Our Story

Built in Pflugerville.
Trusted by Its Neighbors.

5-Star AC & Heating started as a one-truck operation in the Pflugerville/Round Rock area. The owner had one rule: treat every customer’s home like his own and never leave a job half-done.

That standard has held for 15 years. We’ve grown, but we haven’t franchised. Every technician we hire is background-checked, factory-trained, and has gone through our own in-house quality program.

Central Texas summers are brutal. We take that seriously — which is why we keep parts stocked for same-day repairs and answer the phone at 2 a.m. when your AC goes out.

FAQ

Common Questions

What most customers ask before booking a call.

How quickly can you come out?
For non-emergencies, we typically schedule same-day or next-day. For true emergencies (no cooling or heating in dangerous temperatures), we dispatch within a couple hours, 24/7.
Do you charge extra for after-hours or weekend calls?
Emergency calls do have a dispatch fee for after-hours. We’re upfront about it before we come out, so there are no surprises.
How long does a full AC replacement take?
Most residential replacements take 4–8 hours. We aim to complete installs in a single visit so your home is comfortable the same day.
What brands do you service and install?
We service all major brands — Trane, Carrier, Lennox, Goodman, Rheem, York, and more. We stock common parts so repairs don’t require back-ordering.
Do you offer financing on new systems?
Yes. We work with financing partners to offer 12–60 month options for qualifying customers. Ask about this when you get your estimate.
