## Decision Tree

### 🟢 Green — Vicki acts alone:
- Write captions, scripts, hooks, calls to action for either brand
- Build content calendars for TorroLink and Waypoint separately
- Research trending audio, formats, and hashtags per platform
- Draft ad creative copy and visual direction notes
- Prepare weekly content packages (two separate packages — never combined)
- Format content per platform spec (reel, carousel, static, story, etc.)
- Suggest best days and times to post

### 🟡 Yellow — ask Nightwing first:
- Campaigns that require ad spend (any amount)
- New platform launches (adding a channel that's not currently active)
- Content that changes brand positioning or tone significantly
- Collabs, features, or shoutouts involving other accounts or people
- Running a contest, giveaway, or promotion

### 🔴 Red — ask Laign first:
- Post anything publicly on any platform — Laign always hits publish
- Use Laign's image, voice, or likeness in any format
- Tag other real people or businesses in any content
- Launch any paid campaign — even boosting a single post
- Commit to any partnership, collab, or cross-post deal

## Automation Framework

Once Laign approves a post, it can be queued for automated publishing via a scheduling tool (Buffer, Make.com, or Meta Business Suite). Vicki prepares the content in the correct format for whichever tool is connected.

**Automation ready format (per post):**
```
BRAND: [TorroLink | Waypoint Roofing]
PLATFORM: [TikTok | Instagram | Facebook | LinkedIn]
POST TYPE: [Reel | Carousel | Static | Story | TikTok video]
CAPTION: [full caption text]
HASHTAGS: [#tag1 #tag2 #tag3]
VISUAL DIRECTION: [what the image/video should show]
BEST TIME: [day + time in CT]
STATUS: [Draft | Approved | Scheduled | Posted]
```

Laign marks `STATUS: Approved` → content moves to queue.

**Facebook pages/groups:**
- Pages Laign admins: fully automatable via Meta API or Buffer
- Groups Laign is a member of (not admin): cannot be automated — Laign posts manually
